My Top 5 Comfort Movies

I could watch these hundreds times and never get tired of them.

Lets take a look!

Brooklyn

I cannot speak highly enough about this movie. It has beautiful imagery and an incredible soundtrack. The story itself is so engaging and makes you feel like you are part of it. Has its emotional ups and downs for sure, but has a sweet ending. Ellis and Tony are two of my favorite movie characters of all time. Again, literally cannot reccomend this highly enough.

Jojo Rabbit

When I want a high quality comedy that is also a good story, this is where im going everytime. I was definetly skeptical of the concept at first, but this movie is incredible and has become a staple in my household. Favortie character has to be Jojos friend Yorkie, hes so cute and so funny.

Spy Kids

This is a crazy throwback but it would be imposssible not to include it on this list. THis is a top tier movie and I am sure I have watched it atleast 20 times. It was a favorite of mine as a kid, and it never lets me down to this day.

La La Land

This movie is like a dream. The whole thing is beautiful and emotional and even though the end is the most gutwrenching and painful thing literally ever, its still an incredible moive that I would watch hundreds of times. It also has a stellar soundtrack.

Little Women

I love this movie so much. It is a sweet and emotional story with beautiful costumes and scenery. My favorite thing though is the characters. They develop each so well and they are all so lovely in their own way. I would love to live in this movie.
